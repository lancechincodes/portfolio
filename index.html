Book Nook

What it does:

This website allows people to track and rate their favorite study spots by reviewing its noise level, access to outlets, internet speed, and overall ambiance.

Inspiration:

My team and I were inspired to build Book Nook because we realized that many study spots are rated by the quality of their food or drinks on popular apps like Yelp, but not on its quality as a place to study.

Built with:

Book Nook is a full-stack application built with MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js. It also leverages styling tools from Material UI.
